abstract: A decision-support tool for Brazilian legislators to visualize socioeconomic disparities. Aggregates data on health, education, and social assistance to pinpoint municipalities requiring urgent investment and optimize resource distribution.
image: capa08.png
---


**Goal:** Guide Brazilian MPs in distributing resources to municipalities with the highest needs.

**Scope:** Covers indicators for Health, Agriculture, Education, Culture, and Social Assistance.

**Tech:** Built with Power BI to facilitate data-driven policy making. Moreover, index for each area was created in R with the COINr package, following the OECD Handbook on Composite Indicators .

<br>

Access the [Dashboard in the official website of the state of Goiás](https://goias.gov.br/institucional/goias-em-dados/)
